About Us

The Lupon Kagan Islamic Welfare Association, Inc. (LKIWAI), is a non-government organization founded by Elders of the Municipality of Lupon, Davao Oriental, Philippines in September 21, 2011 duly registered to the Philippine Government Security and Exchange Commission (S.E.C.) No. CN201131787.

LKIWAI is also duly accredited and recognized by the Local Government Unit of the Municipality of Lupon, Davao Oriental by the Resolution of the Municipal Council No. 2022-172 in August 22, 2022.

Vision

A progressive and responsible society that maintains peaceful co-existence with other communities.

Mission

  • To preserve and develop the Socio, Cultural, Political and Economic rights of the Kagan communities and integrate them into the mainstream of the society in support of the Indigenous people and other members of the communities through Government Programs and Policies.
  • To establish, develop and support an Integrated Educational Program (Arabic-English Schools) to nurture balance and broad knowledge among the young generation.
  • To receive grants and donations from any local and foreign willing partners.
  • To extend services and help to other people in times of calamities, war conflict and natural and man-made disasters through establishing a responder team in coordination with the government rescue department.
  • To develop, preserve and maintain our Ancestral Domain for the welfare of our future generations.
